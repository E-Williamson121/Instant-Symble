Welcome to Instant Symble, a spin-off of the popular Wordle-like game, Symble. 

In a game of Symble, a hidden word is selected and a set of three unique symbols are chosen at random. 

These symbols are each assigned one of the following three meanings: 

  1. The letter of the hidden word in the symbol's position is in the same spot in the guessed word. 
  2. The letter of the hidden word in the symbol's position is in a different spot in the guessed word. 
  3. The letter of the hidden word in the symbol's position is not in the guessed word.

For example, in the game shown above, 

  • The hidden word is LIPID.
  • The red moon corresponds to the 1st meaning.
  • The pink pentagon corresponds to the 2nd meaning.
  • The purple circle corresponds to the 3rd meaning.

The information of which symbol corresponds to which meaning, and the value of the hidden word, are both not disclosed and must be figured out while solving the puzzle. 

In a game of Instant Symble, you are already given two starting words, and the corresponding symbols are displayed. From the words and symbols given, the solution is narrowed down to a single possibility. 

To win, you must correctly determine this solution.

Good luck.
